Sri Tirumala Bliss

Sri Tirumala Bliss is a premium residential project nestled in Raghavendra Colony, Aramghar Circle, Hyderabad, offering an exceptional blend of modern architecture, serene surroundings, and affordable luxury. This thoughtfully planned community is designed to provide peaceful living while maintaining excellent connectivity to the city's key areas.

From the strategic layout to the elegant interiors, every aspect has been meticulously crafted to deliver homes that offer true value. The project features spacious 3 BHK apartments ranging from 1650 square feet, each designed with modern amenities and quality finishes.

Strategically located near Aramghar Circle, the project provides excellent connectivity to educational institutions, healthcare facilities, shopping centers, and major business districts. The area is well-connected to ORR and major highways, ensuring smooth commutes across Hyderabad.
