Sri Tirumala Golden Pride

Sri Tirumala Golden Pride is a premium residential project strategically located in Mallapur, Near NOMA Convention Centre, Nacharam, Hyderabad. This exceptional development offers the perfect blend of modern architecture, contemporary design, and affordable luxury, making it an ideal choice for families seeking quality living.

From the thoughtful layout to the stunning interiors, every detail has been meticulously crafted to deliver homes that exceed expectations. The project offers spacious 2 BHK and 3 BHK apartments ranging from 1150 to 1850 square feet, each featuring modern amenities and premium finishes.

Strategically positioned near NOMA Convention Centre, the project provides excellent connectivity to schools, hospitals, shopping centers, and major IT hubs. With proximity to Nacharam, ECIL, and major arterial roads, Sri Tirumala Golden Pride ensures hassle-free commutes to all parts of the city.
